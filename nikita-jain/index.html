What I Do

I've been producing video content for 14+ years in newsrooms, startups, and on the road with a camera, chasing the kind of stories that stay with you after the screen goes dark.

How I Work

I direct and produce end-to-end: research, scripting, shoots, interviews, edit, and delivery. I care deeply about technical craft, but my core value is finding the emotional truth in a story and building a narrative that makes people feel something and act.

Experience Snapshot

I've worked with WION, The Indian Express, The Quint, and CNN-News18, and I also create fast, lean content for today's platforms, including phone-shot vertical videos and web-first formats.

What I'm Exploring Now (AI in Workflow)

I'm learning how to use AI in my process, not as a replacement for creativity, but as a way to speed up research, sharpen scripts, test more hooks, and scale storytelling without flattening the human core.

Personal Note

I've been based in Goa since 2020, and I've freelanced with Terry Sent Me!, Rupifi, and Confido Health. I unwind with tennis and drumming, both great training for rhythm, timing, and endurance, which helps in editing too.
